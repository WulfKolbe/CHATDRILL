**Theorem.** Assume $\epsilon > 0$ and let the target network $f$ and its approximation $f_{\epsilon}$ have the same architecture. If every parameter $\theta$ of $f$ and corresponding $\theta_{\epsilon}$ of $f_{\epsilon}$ in layer $l$ fulfils $|\theta_{\epsilon} - \theta| \leq \epsilon_{l}$ for \begin{align} \epsilon_l := \epsilon \left(L \sqrt{m_{l}} \left(1+\sup_{x \in {[-1,1]}^{n_0}} ||\bm{x}^{(l-1)}||_{1}\right) \prod^L_{k=l+1} \left(||\bm{W}^{(l)}||_{\infty} + \epsilon/L \right) \right)^{-1}, \end{align} then it follows that $||f-f_{\epsilon}||_{\infty} \leq \epsilon$.
