**Lemma 3.** Let $h\left(\bm{\theta_0}, \bm{\sigma}\right)$ denote a transformation of the parameters $\bm{\theta_0}$ of the deep neural network $f_0$, where each weight is multiplied by a scalar $\sigma_l$, i.e., $h^{(l)}_{ij}(w^{(l)}_{0,ij}) = \sigma_l w^{(l)}_{0,ij}$, and each bias is transformed to $h^{(l)}_{i} (b^{(l)}_{0,i}) = \prod^l_{m=1}\sigma_m b^{(l)}_{0,i}$. Then, we have $f\left(x \mid h(\bm{\theta_0}, \bm{\sigma})\right) = \prod^L_{l=1} \sigma_l f(x \mid \bm{\theta_0})$.
