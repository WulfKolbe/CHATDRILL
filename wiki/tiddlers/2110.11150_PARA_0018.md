Here, we propose the initialization of nonzero biases, which then become subject to pruning in addition to the network weights.
How should these biases be initialized?
A good approach has to fulfill two essential criteria. 
a) The randomly initialized neural network needs to be trainable by SGD. 
This property is also critical for most pruning algorithms, as they are inspired by SGD and define pruning scores based on gradients.
b) The randomly initialized neural network should contain lottery tickets with high probability.
